About Me

In my lifetime I've worn a whole lot of hats and seen a whole lot of things. I've been fortunate enough to have lived in Scandinavia and Central America, apart from California. I've seen and done a lot over all that time, either as an exchange student in a new country with a completely different language, as an English teacher in Latin America trying to make a go of it in Costa Rica, or managing a bar in Guatemala... The one thing that impressed me the most during my travels is that we're all people with our own struggles. There are no monolithic categories that accurately contain us, and it's fair to expect anything from anyone. It's from this mindset I approach each day, and why I enrolled in and attended the full stack web development bootcamp through the UC Berkeley Extension. I look forward to applying my life's experience and new skills to your team soon...!
